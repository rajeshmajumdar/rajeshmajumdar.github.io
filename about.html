What are we

Cycure Labs is operated by a small team of Cyber Security Researchers in India. Each having expertise in their own fields, forensics, vulnerability assesments, penetration testing, mobile development, python development, web development, cryptography, binary, OSINT, hardware and so on.

Here at Cycure Labs we research on security of different products to find loopholes and make it further more secure.

Apart from Researches

We don't only keep researching into the security, we party, held meetups, provide trainings & develop tools. Including all these we organise a CTF challenge to find new members for our team and we also participates in other CTF challenges too, to keep learning.

Thoughty Thursdays

On every Thursdays of week, we held a small meetup with all the team members and SillyGurus and put our thoughts on the table, to get something productive out of it, we call it as 'Thoughty Thursdays'. Everyone in the team are free to share their thoughts with us.

Want to Join

We'd love to have you in our team, if you want to work with us as a team you just need to solve our CTF challenge we organise every year. We'll choose best of them. Our CTF challenges contains challenges from different fields. So keep yourself prepared.
